Harry Potter Magical Feast

Experience a magical dinner inspired by the wizarding world of Harry Potter.

Appetizer: Snape's Potions Class Potion

Start your magical feast with a mysterious elixir, just like the potions brewed in Snape's class. This concoction includes:

  • 1/2 cup of mandrake root extract
  • 3 bat wings (dried)
  • 1 pinch of powdered bicorn horn
  • Stir it clockwise three times with a wand for an authentic touch.

Main Course: Hogwarts House Feast

Delight in a feast fit for a wizard, featuring dishes representing each Hogwarts house:

  • Gryffindor: Roaring Lion Roast Beef
  • Ravenclaw: Wise Owl Herb-Crusted Chicken
  • Hufflepuff: Badger's Baked Potatoes
  • Slytherin: Serpent's Spicy Green Beans

Dessert: Hagrid's Hagrid-Sized Cake

Indulge in a gigantic cake made from layers of chocolate and pumpkin, just like the one Hagrid would bake. Top it with colorful icing and edible golden snitches.

Cocktail: Dumbledore's Enchanted Elixir

Sip on a mystical elixir that changes colors as you drink it. This enchanted cocktail includes:

  • 1 shot of firewhisky
  • 1/2 shot of unicorn tears
  • 1 dash of Floo Powder for the magical effect
  • Serve it in a crystal goblet.
